BRAND IDENTITY AND PACKAGING

BACKGROUND & ROLE

Salt and Olive is an artisanal oils and Mediterranean food purveyor in Harvard Square. Prior to their opening, I was contacted by the founder to help create turn a desired look into a visual identity and overall brand image.

LOGO DESIGN

During the course of this engagement, I created the logo, playing off a Mediterranean color scheme and using the O as an olive, that is now used on all branding, including the site.

PACKAGING

I also worked directly with their bottling manufacturer to create art for the olive oil bottles that now line the shelves in their store.
